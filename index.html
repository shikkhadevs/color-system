The Usage Strategy

We've introduced a surface scale for UI structure. This ensures our brand colors (Blue, Red, Yellow, Green) are reserved for meaning and interaction.

500

Primary & Brand

Use Blue-500 for primary buttons and active links. Use Blue-50 for hover backgrounds.

500

Success & Growth

Use Green-500 for completion states, verification badges, and positive trends.

500

Attention & Premium

Use Yellow-500 for warnings, "Pro" features, or items requiring user attention.

900

Structure & Text

Use Surface-50 to 900 for everything else. Text should be 600-900, never pure black.
